Key Components:
1. ESP32-C6: Main processor with WiFi 6/Bluetooth 5
2. 8MB PSRAM: Fast memory for menu storage
3. MicroSD: Additional storage for images/orders
4. Touch Display: Interface for business owners
5. Printer Interface: Connects to thermal printers
6. Power Management: Battery backup support
